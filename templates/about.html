About PrepForge

Why We Started

PrepForge began with a simple but frustrating question:

"Why is test prep still stuck in the past?"

As Ivy League students— grinding late nights through static flashcards and recycled practice tests — we saw how broken the traditional system was. Everyone was giving students the same questions, the same pace, the same one-size-fits-all path… regardless of where they were actually struggling.

We didn't need more PDFs.

We needed a smarter way to learn. So we built one.

What We Believe

We believe that every student is different. So your test prep should adapt to you — not the other way around.

At PrepForge, we've created an intelligent learning engine that:

  • Delivers never-repeating, custom questions tailored to your performance
  • Adjusts difficulty in real-time as you improve
  • Helps you study smarter, faster, and more efficiently

No more guesswork.

No more wasted hours.

Just targeted, intelligent progress.

Who We're For

PrepForge is for dreamers who dare to do more:

The GMAT taker pushing for 700+ to land that top MBA admit

The med school hopeful working full-time while prepping for the MCAT

The GRE warrior balancing family, work, and study

The USMLE student who refuses to take shortcuts with their future

Whether you're in a dorm room, on a lunch break, or grinding at midnight — we're here to help you get where you're going.

Our Mission

To make elite-level test preparation affordable, personalized, and accessible for every student in the world — no matter their background, budget, or bandwidth.

You deserve a shot at greatness.

We're here to help you earn it.

From Our Team to You

We're a small but passionate group of educators, engineers, and test-prep survivors who genuinely care about leveling the playing field. Whether you're applying to business school, med school, or grad school — your success is personal to us.

If you're reading this, we want you to know:

You're not alone.

You can do this.

And we built PrepForge to prove it.

Let's get to work.

— The PrepForge Team
